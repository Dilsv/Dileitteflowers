Care Instructions

Caring for a flower bouquet involves several steps to ensure its longevity and freshness. Upon receiving the bouquet, trim the stems at an angle with a sharp knife or scissors to create a fresh surface for water absorption. Remove any foliage that would be submerged in water to prevent bacterial growth. Place the bouquet in a clean vase filled with lukewarm water and a packet of flower food, following the instructions on the packet. Keep the bouquet away from direct sunlight and heat sources, as excessive warmth can accelerate wilting. Change the water every 2-3 days, recutting the stems each time to maintain water uptake. Remove any wilted or spent flowers promptly to encourage the remaining blooms to flourish. Additionally, ensure the bouquet is kept in a draft-free area to prevent dehydration. Following these steps will help extend the vibrancy and beauty of your flower bouquet.
